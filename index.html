Ashley was born in Hamilton, Ontario and adopted by the Moras Family. She grew up in Oakville with 3 siblings, an older sister and younger brother, also adopted, and a younger sister, who is her parents' biological child.

At a young age, Ashley discovered a love of dance and began her professional training to become a ballet dancer at the age of 10. She trained professionally for over 8 years, attending the exclusive Summer Dance Program as part of the Banff Arts Festival, for 4 summers in a row.
